# How to Contribute

You can contribute to this project by:

* Helping solve open questions raised in videos
* Identifying mistakes or inconsistencies in calculations
* Suggesting improvements to models or assumptions
* Providing references or verified game mechanics

Contributions should be:

* Clear and structured
* Based on verifiable Minecraft mechanics
* Supported by evidence when possible
